## Use Cases

1. **Humorous UX Feedback**: Get entertaining yet insightful critiques of your website design from an AI with a sharp wit, making feedback more engaging than traditional audits.

2. **Viral Marketing Tool**: Create shareable, humorous content by roasting your own or competitors' websites, driving engagement and awareness.

3. **Ice Breaker for Design Reviews**: Use AI roasts to kick off serious design discussions with humor, making teams more receptive to criticism.

4. **Community Engagement**: Build community around design feedback by having users submit websites for public roasting, creating entertaining content.
